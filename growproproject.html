    • GrowPro assisted farming app

    Growpro offers urban farmers an opportunity to control their farm from virtually anywhere via an app on their smartphone that is linked to their small container farm. Their urban farm will make use of the latest technologies such as hydroponics, LED lighting and humidity controllers. All this can be controlled at the click of a button.

    The interface features a green bold style with clear content hierarchy that allows for a smooth user flow. The main point our team found when researching our target market was that the app needed to be easily understandable and as simple as possible to allow all age demographics to access and utilize it.
