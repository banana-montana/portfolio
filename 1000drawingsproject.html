    • Night of 1000 Drawings Marketing Campaign

    1000 Drawings is a charity event in Cape Town that involves doodling for a good cause. Members of the public are invited to doodle session events to doodle a drawing and at the end of the year all of the doodles are auctioned off and the proceeds go to charity. I worked on a team of four to create a complete rebrand for the event as well as 2017's marketing campaign.

    My role in the team was to assist in conceptualizing for the final idea and to create the graphics used (illustrations, poster layouts, doodles used on the items, etc.). This has been one of the most exciting projects I've worked on and the hands on approach of it taught me a lot about marketing.
